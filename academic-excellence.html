Learning at Sunshine

Where curiosity meets creativity

At Sunshine Child-Care Nursery, we believe learning should be joyful, engaging, and tailored to each child's unique needs. Our holistic approach combines play-based activities with structured learning to nurture confidence, creativity, and a love for discovery.

Our Learning Approach

Play-Based Learning

Children explore, experiment, and learn through hands-on experiences.

Early Literacy & Numeracy

Building strong foundations for future success.

Creative Arts & Music

Encouraging imagination and self-expression.

Outdoor Exploration

Nature-based activities for physical and sensory development.

Social & Emotional Growth

Helping children develop confidence and empathy.

Daily Learning Highlights

  • Circle Time Songs, stories, and sharing moments.
  • Small Group Activities Focused learning in a fun, interactive way.
  • Outdoor Play Physical development and nature discovery.
  • Creative Corners Art, music, and role play to spark creativity.

A Child's Day at Sunshine

Every day at Sunshine Child-Care Nursery is thoughtfully planned to balance learning, play, and care. Here's what a typical day looks like:

Breakfast Club

07:30 – 09:00 AM

  • Arrival & Warm Welcome – Children are greeted by friendly staff and settle in comfortably.
  • Nutritious Breakfast – A healthy meal to start the day right.
  • Quiet Activities – Gentle play, reading corners, and creative table-top activities to ease into the morning.

Preschool Session

09:00 AM – 15:00 PM

  • Circle Time – Songs, stories, and sharing to build confidence and social skills.
  • Learning Through Play – Structured activities focusing on early literacy, numeracy, and creative arts.
  • Outdoor Exploration – Time in the garden for physical play and nature discovery.
  • Healthy Lunch & Rest – Balanced meals followed by quiet time or naps for younger children.
  • Afternoon Activities – Sensory play, role play, and group projects to encourage curiosity and teamwork.

After School Club

15:00 PM – 18:00 PM

  • Relax & Recharge – A calm environment for children after a busy school day.
  • Snack Time – Light refreshments to keep energy up.
  • Fun & Creative Play – Arts, crafts, games, and outdoor play to end the day on a happy note.
  • Home Time – Parents receive updates through live journals and friendly handovers.
