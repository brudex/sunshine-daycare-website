About Sunshine Child-Care Nursery

Over 30 years of nurturing young minds and building tomorrow's leaders.

Our Story

For more than three decades, Sunshine has been a trusted part of Rainham's community, providing exceptional early years education. Many of our alumni now serve in leadership roles across the country, a testament to our commitment to excellence.

At Sunshine Child-Care Nursery, we believe every child deserves a safe, joyful, and inspiring start. Located in Rainham, Kent, we provide high-quality early years education in a warm, nurturing environment. For over 30 years, Sunshine has been a trusted part of the community, helping children grow into confident, capable individuals. Many of our former pupils are now leaders and professionals making a positive impact in our society and across the country. Our legacy is built on care, learning, and strong family partnerships.

Community & Partnership

Working hand-in-hand with families.

Trust grows through transparent, joyful communication

We believe families are at the heart of a child's success. Through key person relationships, live learning journals, and open classrooms, we keep parents connected and involved every step of the way. Together, we celebrate milestones and create a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

  • Key Person Relationships – Dedicated staff member for each child
  • Live Learning Journals – Real-time updates on your child's day
  • Open Classrooms – Transparent, welcoming environment
